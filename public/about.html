About Alex Ashtiany

I’ve been training clients for over 15 years — athletes, professionals, and parents — helping them move better, get stronger, and build confidence that lasts outside the gym.

I hold certifications from the National Personal Training Institute (NPTI), as well as in Nutrition Coaching and Kettlebell Training.

My approach is simple: no gimmicks, no shortcuts, no wasted time. Every program is built on principles of strength, movement efficiency, and consistency. My goal isn’t just to make you stronger — it’s to teach you how to be self-reliant, confident, and disciplined for life.

Photos: powerlifting meet, half marathon podium, and HYROX finish.

Background

I started with pure strength—barbell basics—then expanded into hybrid conditioning. I test the protocols I prescribe: meets, races, and events. The result is programming that’s efficient, proven, and built for busy schedules.

I coach clients to move pain-free, build muscle, drop fat, and perform. We keep what works. We cut what doesn’t. No fluff.

Philosophy

Training should make you independent, not dependent on me. You’ll learn the skill of training: technique, progression, recovery, and intent. Strong body. Strong mind. Strong standards.

  • Simple lifts done right—progress tracked, form dialed.
  • Conditioning that matters—carryover to sport and life.
  • Systems you can run on your own—wherever you train.

Mission

Build healthier, stronger people—and teach them to **own** the process. Confidence in the gym, confidence in life. That’s the target.
